Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you organize?

We organize weddings, corporate events, birthdays, themed parties, and other special occasions with personalized touches.

How can I book your services?

Simply fill out the contact form on our website or call us directly. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your needs.

Do you provide customized event planning?

Yes, we provide fully customized event planning to ensure your event is unique and memorable.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the local area but are available for travel upon request. Contact us for details!

Can you handle last-minute events?

We do our best to accommodate last-minute events. Contact us as soon as possible to check our availability.

What is included in your event planning services?

We offer venue selection, décor, catering, entertainment, and day-of coordination to ensure a seamless experience.

Do you offer event styling and design?

Yes! We offer complete event styling and design to bring your vision to life and make your event truly special.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance to secure your date and ensure thorough planning.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that circumstances change. Contact us for full details on our flexible cancellation and refund policy.
